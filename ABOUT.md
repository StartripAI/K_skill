# About K.skill

## About K.skill

K.skill is a personal, fully-local product for reopening the people you care about. It helps you reconstruct someone's way of talking, the sound of their voice, the photos you kept of them, and now a short talking-portrait video — gathered into one place that stays on your own device. It is built for anyone holding onto a parent, a partner, a friend, or a grandparent, and who wants to keep that presence close in a form they can return to.

The flow is simple and deliberate. You begin by importing a history of conversations you already have — the everyday back-and-forth that carries how a person actually speaks. From that material, K.skill distills a persona: the rhythms, the recurring phrases, the way they answered a question or changed the subject. With a sample of their voice, it then generates speech that sounds like them rather than a stranger reading their words. A single clear photo becomes the basis for a talking portrait, rendered so the face moves with what is being said. Finally, everything — persona, voice, images, and video — is carried in a portable pack: a single file you own, can move between machines, and can keep for as long as you want.

Privacy is not a setting here; it is the design. K.skill runs entirely on your hardware. The conversations you import, the voice you reconstruct, the faces you render — none of it is sent anywhere. There is no account to create, no upload step, no server holding a copy. What you build stays with you, which is the only arrangement that makes sense for material this personal.

K.skill also meets your hardware where it is. On a phone-class browser it gives you the core experience — a persona you can talk with and a voice you can hear. As you move to a laptop and then a workstation, it unlocks richer media: longer and higher-fidelity audio, and the talking-portrait video that needs more room to render. The product adapts to what your device can do, so the same pack can grow with you over time without ever leaving the place you keep it.

## 关于 K.skill

K.skill 是一款完全在本地运行的个人产品，用来重新打开你所珍视的人。它帮你重建一个人说话的方式、声音的质感、你留存的照片，以及现在的一段会说话的肖像视频——把这一切汇聚到一处，并始终留在你自己的设备上。它为每一个牵挂着父母、伴侣、朋友或祖辈的人而做，让那份存在以一种可以反复回到的形式留在身边。

它的流程清晰而有分寸。你先导入一段你已经拥有的对话记录——那些日常的你来我往，承载着一个人真实的说话方式。K.skill 从这些材料中提炼出一个人格：他的节奏、反复出现的口头语、回答问题或转移话题的习惯。再借助一小段他的声音样本，它生成的语音听起来就是他本人，而不是一个陌生人在念他的字句。一张清晰的照片成为会说话肖像的基础，画面经过渲染，让面容随着话语自然地动起来。最后，一切——人格、声音、照片与视频——都收进一个可携带的包里：一个属于你的单一文件，可以在不同设备之间移动，并按你的意愿长久保存。

在这里，隐私不是一项可选设置，而是设计本身。K.skill 完全在你的硬件上运行。你导入的对话、你重建的声音、你渲染的面容——没有任何一项会被传送到别处。无需注册账号，没有上传步骤，也没有任何服务器保留一份副本。你所构建的一切都只属于你——对于如此私人的材料，这是唯一说得通的安排。

K.skill 也会顺应你设备的能力。在手机级别的浏览器上，它提供核心体验——一个可以对话的人格，一段可以听见的声音。当你换到笔记本、再到工作站，它会解锁更丰富的媒体：更长、更高保真的音频，以及需要更多算力来渲染的会说话肖像视频。产品会随着设备的能力而调整，于是同一个包能够伴你慢慢成长，却始终不必离开你存放它的地方。
